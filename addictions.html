Addictions/Inner city clinic

o Clinic at HSC that anyone admitted with SUD complications (especially with injection drug use and severe AUD) can be referred to and seen on a rapid basis. o Start-off by focusing on Naltrexone and PrEP and expand care as needed. o Have clinic run 4 days/week. One day at HSC, the other 3 days in the community such as at Sunshine House, Main Street Project, Bell Hotel etc. o Multiple clinicians would be part of the clinic and ideally people would work a week at a time (similar to HIV clinic). o Provide Harm Reduction services o Would ideally have nurse, social worker, Indigenous Elder, People with lived experience and academic researcher associated with clinic. o Residents could rotate through these clinics as well. o Training in R5 year is required, details TBD.
